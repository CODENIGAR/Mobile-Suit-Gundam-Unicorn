RX-0 Unicorn Gundam 03 Phenex

The RX-0 Unicorn Gundam 03 Phenex[1] (aka Phenex) is a mobile suit introduced in Mobile Suit Gundam UC: One of Seventy Two.
It also appeared in the manga Mobile Suit Gundam U.C. 0096: Last Sun and the Mobile Suit Gundam Unicorn novel.
It made its animated debut in the movie Mobile Suit Gundam Narrative. Known pilots included Rita Bernal and Jona Basta.
