MSA-005K Guncannon Detector

The MSA-005K Guncannon Detector (often misspelled as "Dictector", and also known as"Guncannon DT")
is a mobile suit developed from the MSA-005 Methuss, and introduced in Z-MSV. Its design was later updated for the
Mobile Suit Gundam Unicorn novel, its OVA adaptation and the television re-cut.
