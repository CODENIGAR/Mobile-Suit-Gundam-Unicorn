RX-0 Full Armor Unicorn Gundam Plan B

The RX-0 Full Armor Unicorn Gundam Plan B (also known as Another Full Armor) was the RX-0 Unicorn Gundam with all the Armed Armor equipment.
It was known as RX-0 Unicorn Gundam Perfectibility when its Armed Armor DE units featured stabilizers from RX-0 Unicorn Gundam 03 Phenex (Narrative Ver.).
When it further incorporated equipment from RX-0 Full Armor Unicorn Gundam, it was known as RX-0 Unicorn Gundam Perfectibility Divine.
It first appeared in the Gundam Try Age game and later in Mobile Suit Gundam UC Perfectibility.
